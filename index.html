Hi — I'm Jannatul Ferdous

I am a Lecturer and Researcher in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the International University of Business Agriculture and Technology (IUBAT).

With over two years of academic experience, my work focuses on advancing intelligent systems through cutting-edge research in machine learning, deep learning, federated learning, and explainable AI (XAI). My research contributions span diverse and impactful domains, including 6G wireless communication with blockchain integration, smart city technologies, healthcare AI, energy systems, and environmental analytics.

I am particularly interested in developing communication-efficient and privacy-preserving AI systems, as well as interpretable models for real-world decision-making. My ongoing research includes reinforcement learning-based optimization, multi-agent systems, and hybrid ensemble approaches for complex problem solving.

Beyond research, I am deeply committed to mentorship and academic development. I have successfully supervised numerous undergraduate theses and practicum projects in areas such as deep learning, computer vision, and intelligent systems, guiding students toward both academic and applied success.
